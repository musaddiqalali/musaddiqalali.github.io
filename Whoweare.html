Who we are

Interdisciplinary scientists and engineers who enjoy challenging endeavors

Dr. Musaddiq Al Ali Google Scholar

We're a group of interdisciplinary scientists and engineers who are passionate about computational modeling and engineering design optimization. Modern structures are built with numerous functions in mind, necessitating the use of design methods that can handle multiscale and multiphysics issues. For this goal, our team develops and deploys cutting-edge optimization methods, focusing on difficulties such as large-scale design variables, global optimization, nonlinearity, complex physics coupling, and high computational cost. The group collaborates with diverse researchers at the intersection of engineering, computer science, and applied mathematics to provide simple and effective engineering solutions.
