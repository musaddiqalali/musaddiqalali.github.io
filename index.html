Grant for research excellence from Toyota foundation: commemorating the 40th anniversary of Toyota technological institute

2021
  • I was awarded the research grant of the 40th anniversary of Toyota Technological Institute in 2021, and Professor Dr. Shimoda and I embarked on groundbreaking research endeavors. this grant, amounting to 10 million yen, enabled us to delve into the realm of designing porous metamaterials with anisotropic heat conductivity. Notably, our collaborative efforts led to the development of a pioneering design methodology for such materials, marking a significant milestone in the field. Subsequently, our research findings were validated by an independent research group in China which published the experimental result in nature communications, further affirming the novelty and impact of our contributions to the scientific community.
