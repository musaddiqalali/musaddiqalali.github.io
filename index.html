About

Hi, I'm Musaddiq. I am a trilingual Iraqi/Japanese multidisciplinary engineer with expertise in numerical methods, finite element analysis, and optimization techniques. With a Ph.D. in Mechanical Engineering, Structural Innovation and Optimization, I have gained valuable experience in mechanical design, heat transfer, and piezoelectric devices. I have worked in several capacities, including as a post-doctoral researcher at Toyota Technological Institute-Nagoy-Japan and as a design and validation engineer at SHARP Corporation. My experience also includes teaching and mentoring under- and post-graduate students in various porjects. I am proficient in programming languages such as MATLAB,VBA,and Python and have a working knowledge of CAD software. Additionally, I possess excellent communication and interpersonal skills and can effectively communicate complex technical information to diverse audiences.
